Mods

Your robot ant can be programmed in many languages: OCaml, Python, C, C++, Java, Ruby, Lua, JavaScript, Pascal, Perl, Scheme, Vala, Prolog. Experienced programmers may also add their own favorite language.

Translation

Laby is available in Czech, Danish, German, English, Spanish, French, Hungarian, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Albanian, Slovakian, Turkish, Ukrainian, and more.
